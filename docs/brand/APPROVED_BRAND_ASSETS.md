| Asset | Use |
|---|---|

## Palette

| Token | Hex | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| Paper | `#f6f5f2` | App background and mark plate |
| Ink | `#1e1d1a` | Primary dark field and text |
| Trust blue | `#2457d6` | SignalOS trust and endorsement |
| Signal orange | `#d97845` | Active build signal |
| Soft blue | `#b7c9ff` | Dark-surface endorsement text |

## Usage Rules

- Use the mark alone for app icon, sidebar icon, and compact navigation.
- Use the wordmark lockup on onboarding, splash, docs, and release pages.
- Do not place the mark inside another rounded card.
- Do not recolor the mark with purple, brown, or neon accents.
- Do not use the old SignalOS ring mark for Foundry-facing surfaces.
