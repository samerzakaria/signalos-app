Foundry by SignalOS identity concepts

A tighter direction for a premium governed product studio: a built F, a controlled frame, and one active teal signal. No literal flame, no robot, no terminal mark, no generic AI glyph.

Design decision: the mark should be a constructed monogram, not an illustration. Foundry needs to feel like a serious product studio with governance built in, so the frame and F do the work while the teal signal marks active build state.

Recommended route: Mark 01. It is the most ownable, scales cleanly, and can survive as an app icon without the wordmark.
